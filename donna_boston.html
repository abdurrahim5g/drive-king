DONNA BOSTON




Program Manager


Donna Boston, engineer/interdisciplinary scientist, is a Program Manager with BARDA’s Division of Research, Innovation and Ventures at the US Department of Health and Human Services. Her work focuses on innovative products and technologies that will transform health security, including novel vaccine approaches and breakthrough diagnostic solutions. Donna has directed diagnostics and detection programs with BARDA and with the Department of Defense’s Chem/Bio Defense program for over thirty years as a certified member of the acquisition workforce and Army Acquisition Corps, and is a senior fellow of the Excellence in Government program. She earned chemical and biomedical engineering degrees from Johns Hopkins and the University of Maryland. Her experience includes developing in vitro diagnostics and chemical detection systems, and identifying and accelerating early stage companies to commercialization.
